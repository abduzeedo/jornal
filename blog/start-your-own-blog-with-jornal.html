Start Your Own Blog with Jornal

A simple guide for anyone who wants their own blog. No coding. No technical stuff. Just you and Claude.

You're reading this blog right now. You like it. You want one. Here's the thing: you can have your own version running in minutes, and you don't need to understand anything technical.

All you need is Claude Code. It handles everything else.

What You Need

  • A free GitHub account (this is where your blog will live)
  • Claude Code installed on your computer

That's it. Let's go.

Getting Started

Open Claude Code and tell it:

"Start my blog using this template: github.com/abduzeedo/jornal"

Claude will take care of everything: creating your own copy of the blog, downloading it to your computer, and setting it up. Just follow along and say yes when it asks for permission.

When it's done, you'll have your blog ready to customize.

Make It Yours

Now the fun part. Just tell Claude what you want:

  • "Change the site name to [your name]'s Blog"
  • "Update the about page—here's a bit about me: [your bio]"
  • "Change the accent color to green"
  • "Add my email to the contact page"

Claude makes the changes. You see them happen. Simple.

See It Live on Your Computer

Want to see how your blog looks? Just say:

"Show me the blog locally"

Claude will start a preview and give you a link to open in your browser. You can see your changes before anyone else does.

Write Your First Post

Ready to write? Just say:

"Let's blog"

Then describe what you want to write about. Give Claude a topic, a few key points, the tone you want. It'll write a draft for you. Don't like something? Just say "make the intro shorter" or "add a section about X."

When you're happy with it, Claude creates the post and updates your blog automatically.

Put It on the Internet

Your blog only lives on your computer right now. To share it with the world, say:

"Publish my blog"

Claude will upload your changes to GitHub and enable hosting. In a minute or two, your blog will be live at an address like yourusername.github.io/jornal.

That's it. Your blog is on the internet. For free.

The Daily Workflow

Once everything is set up, your routine is simple:

  1. Open Claude Code in your blog folder
  2. Say "let's blog" and describe your post
  3. Review and tweak until you're happy
  4. Say "publish" to put it online

No complicated dashboards. No fighting with formatting tools. Just a conversation.

What You're Getting

  • Free hosting — GitHub Pages doesn't cost anything
  • Fast loading — No slow databases, just instant pages
  • Total control — It's your site, customize anything
  • No maintenance — Nothing to update or patch
  • AI-powered — Claude helps you write and manage everything

Just Start

You don't need to understand how websites work. You don't need to learn to code. You just need to know what you want to say.

Open Claude Code. Tell it to start your blog. Then start writing.
